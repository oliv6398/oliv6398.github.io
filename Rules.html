Unwritten Rules

In Denmark, as well as other contries, we have a lot of unwritten rules. Here are a few, that is important to know when traveling to Denmark

Norms that are nice to know

  • We say thanks a lot. After we have eaten. When someone holds the door for you. If someone helps you. And the list goes on

  • We use sacastic humor all the time. When someone is saying something bad, remember it is probably sacastic.

  • We dont talk to strangers. When danes use pupblic transport or something else, they only speak to the people they know. It is very strange to a Dane, if a stranger talks to him.

  • In addition to the one above: If you get on the puplic transport, don't choose a seat next to someone. Always go for the seat with the least amount of people around it.

  • We don't wear shoes indoores. Just don't do it :)

  • We talk a lot about the weather. Whether or not it is good.

  • We use bicycles quite a lot - especially in Copenhagen. Many people takes their bikes to work everyday.
